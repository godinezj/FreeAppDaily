Free App Daily
Everybody likes free apps, but not as much as they love free pay-for apps. Free App Daily is a simple application helps you keep tabs on the Amazon Appstore by notifying you when a new pay-for app is released for free (every day). You can hit the notification and go directly to the Amazon Appstore and install the app directly onto your device. I hope you like my free app, feel free to send me feedback or feature requests.
